## what to refresh

**Quarterly (per batch):**
- Application deadline in `yc-context.md`
- RFS list in `yc-context.md`
- Verify application form questions match `application-questions.md`

**Annually:**
- Deal terms in `yc-context.md`
- Partner roster
- Acceptance rate stats
- Batch size

**On major change:**
- PG essay if rewritten (unlikely - stable since ~2018)
- 4-batch structure if changed
- Big partner reshuffle

## what NOT trust forever

- Spring 2026 RFS specifics - staled Q3 2026
- Specific deadline dates - stale every batch
- "Current as of 2025-2026" framing - re-date annually
- Acceptance rate - drift over time
- Garry Tan's specific quotes - fresh as of late 2025, stale by 2027
